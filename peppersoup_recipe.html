Ingredients

  • Any protein of choice (goat meat, beef, cat-fish or chicken)
  • (Ground pepper soup spices)
  • Scotch bonnet peppers (ata rodo), chopped
  • Chopped onions
  • Minced ginger and garlic
  • Seasoning cubes or powder
  • Salt to taste
  • Crayfish, ground
  • Scent leaves
  • 6 cups water

Instructions

  1. Wash and cut the protein into desired piece. Place them in a large pot.
  2. Add the chopped onion, garlic, ginger, and Scotch bonnet peppers to the pot.
  3. Season with the ground pepper soup spices, seasoning cubes or powder, and salt. Stir to combine.
  4. Pour in the water, ensuring that the protein is fully covered. Bring the mixture to a boil over medium heat.
  5. Once boiling, reduce the heat to low and simmer the soup for 45 minutes to 1 hour, or until the protein is tender.
  6. Stir in the ground crayfish and continue to cook for another 5-10 minutes.
  7. Proceed with scent leaf, add to the pot in the final few minutes of cooking.
  8. Adjust seasoning to taste, adding more salt or pepper if necessary.
  9. Serve the Pepper Soup hot, either on its own or with a side of boiled plantains, yams, or rice.
