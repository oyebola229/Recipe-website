Ingredients

  • Ground Egusi (melon seeds)
  • Palm oil
  • Chopped onion
  • Minced garlic
  • Cray fish
  • Ground pepper
  • Iru (locust beans)
  • Seasoning cube or powder
  • Salt
  • Smoked fish
  • Assorted meat
  • Ponmo (cow skin)
  • Ugu
  • Ground dry pepper
  • Meat broth/water

Instructions

  1. Heat the palm oil in a large pot over medium heat. Add the chopped onions and saute.
  2. Add minced garlic and ground pepper. Stir and cook for another minute.
  3. Mix the ground egusi with a small amount of water to form a thick paste, then add it to the pot. Stir continuously to prevent burning and cook for 5 minutes.
  4. Add ground cray fish, iru, and seasoning cube or powder.
  5. Pour in the meat broth, and bring the mixture to a boil.
  6. Add the assorted meat, cow skin, and smoked fish. Stir to combine.
  7. Reduce the heat and simmer for 10-20 minutes, allowing the flavors to blend and the soup to thicken.
  8. Add the chopped ugu leaves and simmer for an additional 5 minutes.
  9. Adjust the seasoning with salt and more pepper, if needed.
  10. Serve hot with any swallow of your choice. (Pounded yam, eba, fufu, etc).
