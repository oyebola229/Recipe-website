Ingredients

  • Peeled beans (black-eyed peas or brown beans)
  • Onion, chopped
  • Scotch bonnet peppers (ata rodo), chopped
  • Salt to taste
  • Vegetable oil for frying
  • Water (as needed for blending)

Instructions

  1. Soak the peeled beans in water for about 1 hour to soften. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a blender, combine the soaked beans, chopped onion, and Scotch bonnet peppers. Add a small amount of water, just enough to blend into a smooth, thick batter.
  3. Transfer the blended mixture into a large bowl. Add salt to taste and stir the batter thoroughly to incorporate air, making it fluffy.
  4. Heat vegetable oil in a deep frying pan or pot over medium heat.
  5. Once the oil is hot, scoop the batter using a tablespoon and carefully drop it into the hot oil.
  6. Fry the Akara in batches, making sure not to overcrowd the pan. Fry until golden brown on all sides, turning as needed.
  7. Remove the fried Akara from the oil and drain on paper towels to remove excess oil.
  8. Serve hot with pap (ogi), bread, or as a snack.
