Ingredients

  • Peeled beans
  • Red bell peppers
  • Chopped onions
  • Ginger
  • Cray fish
  • Vegetable oil
  • Seasoning cubes or ppowder
  • Salt to taste
  • Boilled eggs
  • Smoked fish
  • Water (as needed for blending and mixing)
  • Moi-Moi wraps

Instructions

  1. Soak the peeled beans in water for about 1 hour to soften. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a blender, combine the soaked beans, red bell pepper, onion, ginger, and crayfish. Add enough water to blend into a smooth batter.
  3. Transfer the blended mixture into a large mixing bowl. Add the ground pepper, vegetable oil, seasoning cube or powder, and salt. Mix well until everything is evenly combined.
  4. If using boiled eggs or smoked fish, gently fold them into the batter.
  5. Prepare your Moi Moi wraps or containers by greasing them lightly. Pour the batter into the wraps or containers, filling them about three-quarters full.
  6. Arrange the filled wraps or containers in a large pot or steamer. Add enough water to steam (make sure the water doesn't touch the Moi Moi).
  7. Cover the pot tightly and steam the Moi Moi on medium heat for 45 minutes to 1 hour, or until it is set and cooked through.
  8. Check the water level occasionally and add more if needed to prevent burning.
  9. Once cooked, remove the Moi Moi from the pot and allow it to cool slightly before serving.
  10. Serve warm as a side dish or a main meal with rice, bread, or your preferred accompaniment.
